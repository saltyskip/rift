# Demo Asset Prompts

These prompts capture the intended creative direction for the checked-in demo theme assets.

## nord-roast

- Mood: warm editorial coffee brand, tactile, premium but cozy.
- Hero prompt: "Pour-over coffee editorial still life on parchment-toned paper, warm roast palette, ceramic dripper, copper accents, soft natural morning light, magazine-quality composition."
- Logo prompt: "Minimal wax-seal inspired coffee brand mark, monogram, earthy copper and roast brown on cream."

## volt-run

- Mood: kinetic, urban, high-performance night training brand.
- Hero prompt: "Athletic sprint scene with motion blur and neon lime + cyan streaks, dramatic dark backdrop, futuristic running campaign art."
- Logo prompt: "Sharp geometric lightning-bolt running brand symbol, black background, neon lime accent, modern sports identity."

## atelier-stay

- Mood: quiet luxury hospitality, airy, editorial, restrained.
- Hero prompt: "Bright luxury suite with soft morning light, limestone, linen, pale oak and brushed brass details, boutique hotel campaign image."
- Logo prompt: "Elegant hospitality crest with refined serif sensibility, navy and muted gold, understated premium branding."
